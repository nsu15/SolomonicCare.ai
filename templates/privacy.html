Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025
Last Updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyBestFriend.ai (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We are committed to respecting your privacy while delivering compassionate AI-driven mental health support. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information.

2. What We Collect

  • User Input Data:
    - Text-based conversations between users and our AI
    - Self-reported emotional states, requests, and responses
  • Device & Usage Data:
    - IP address, browser type, interaction timestamps
    - Non-identifiable metadata related to platform usage
  • Location Data (if enabled by user):
    - Used to suggest local mental health resources when requested

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide AI responses tailored to user input
  • Suggest nearby or relevant resources based on user requests
  • Improve service quality, responsiveness, and emotional relevance
  • Generate de-identified, aggregated insights for research and development

4. Sponsored & Suggested Resources

When a user opts in, we may show sponsored or suggested partners, such as:

  • Local clinics, hospitals, or nonprofit organizations
  • Free or sliding-scale mental health services
  • White-labeled partner versions of MyBestFriend.ai

These suggestions are never shown without user intent and are matched based on context, not user identity.

5. De-Identified Data Sharing

We may aggregate and anonymize data to share insights with:

  • Public health agencies
  • Mental health researchers
  • Nonprofit funders
  • Policy developers
  • Technology partners

We never sell personal data. All shared data is stripped of personal identifiers and used solely for the advancement of mental health outcomes.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your data. No system is impenetrable, but we work continuously to ensure high standards of data protection.

7. Your Rights

  • Request to see or delete your data
  • Opt out of intent-based suggestions
  • Limit or disable location access
